About Us

Furry Friends Training and Care was established in 2020, by a team of members dedicated to looking after dogs. Located in Blanchardstown, we offer an array of great services such, grooming and dog minding. With state of the art facilities, we are sure that your dog will love it here and will receive the best care. Why not contact us today and give yourself the peace of mind you deserve while you're away from your dog.
